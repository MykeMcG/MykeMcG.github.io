SE-Bonus Locations

Here is a list of the SE-bonus items I've found so far. It is by no means a complete list, as I've yet to find them all, but I figure someone may find this list useful.

Stage 1-1

Right at the very start of stage 1-1, a flock of birds flies off into the distance. One of these birds will be orange. Shoot it, and it will drop a SE-bonus.

During the fight with Arc Adder, once you've destroyed its shields, it will start firing rings of energy at you. With some careful maneuvering, it's possible to fly through these rings. Fly through four rings, and Arc Adder will drop a SE-bonus when you defeat it.

I know of a bunch more, but haven't found the time to gather the necessary screenshots. I'll keep adding to this list as I find time.
